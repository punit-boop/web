Our People

Meet the Team

The dedicated individuals driving change and serving communities across India.

Sh. Radhey Shyam

Hon'ble Chairperson
Arun-Kumar Foundation (AKF)

I am Radhey Shyam, the Chairperson and a retired Headmaster. Hailing from Mirchpur, Haryana-a village renowned for producing a teacher from nearly every home-I have dedicated my life to rural education, character building, and community service. Throughout my career, I have remained committed to empowering students and strengthening society through the transformative power of education. Even after retirement, I continue to serve with the same passion and dedication, striving to inspire future generations through leadership, values, and lifelong service.

Dr. Arun Kumar

Founder & Director
Arun-Kumar Foundation (AKF)

I am Dr. Arun Kumar, Founder of the Arun-Kumar Foundation. As a social reformer and changemaker, I have dedicated my journey to serving society through impactful initiatives in healthcare, education, youth leadership, and community development. It is an honor to have been recognized with awards such as the APJ Abdul Kalam Sewa Samman, but my true motivation lies in creating meaningful change. Through the Arun-Kumar Foundation, I remain committed to building a just, empowered, and compassionate India where every individual has the opportunity to grow, contribute, and thrive.

Arun-Kumar Foundation (AKF)

I'm Aryan Kharb, Chief Coordinator at the Arun-Kumar Foundation. I don't build for applause, I build because the work needs to get done. My focus is education, empowerment, and digital outreach - quiet work, real impact. I believe in a just, awakened India. Not the one we talk about. The one we actually build - brick by brick, without waiting for permission, without needing an audience. That's the only kind of work I trust.

Kabir Sharma

Arun-Kumar Foundation (AKF)

I am a Global Learning & Development and Organization Development Leader passionate about driving workforce capability across complex, multi-business healthcare ecosystems. I specialize in architecting strategies that align employee growth with critical business objectives.
