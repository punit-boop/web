Social Internship

Open for All Students

Serve Society, Grow as a Changemaker

Our Social Internship program gives students a chance to step beyond classrooms and serve real communities. It is fully remote, meaningful, and recognized for school and college submissions.

  • Fully Remote — work from anywhere in India
  • Valid for school & college portfolio submissions
  • Certificate from AKF on completion
  • Real community projects, not busywork
  • Mentorship from AKF leadership

Education to All

For Every Child, Everywhere

Quality Education Knows No Boundary

We believe every child deserves quality education regardless of where they are born. Our Education to All program brings sustainable, skills-based learning to underserved communities.

  • Coding, Graphic Design & Structural Studies
  • Slum & rural area outreach (Shiksha Samarth)
  • Educational kit distribution
  • Multiple learning tracks for different needs
  • Students only — focused on youth development

Healthcare Accessible to All

Good health is a right, not a privilege. AKF's Healthcare sector runs camps, awareness drives, and support services to bring basic healthcare to underserved communities.

  • Minor health check-up camps in rural areas
  • Cancer awareness drives & early detection
  • Fit India Movement — obesity & wellness
  • Community health education programs
  • Partnerships with medical professionals
